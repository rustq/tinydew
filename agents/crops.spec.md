# Crops Spec

## Status
Implemented.

## Crop Types
- Carrot
- Strawberry
- Cauliflower
- Rhubarb

## Behavior
- Crops are planted from seeds on valid farm tiles.
- Growth depends on watered-day progression and day transitions.
- Mature crops can be harvested into produce inventory.
- Crops can be sold through trade/MCP sell commands.
